What we're building

An AI conversation partner that starts from where you actually are.

Kin Lantern is designed for second-generation heritage learners first — not people learning Mandarin as a foreign language, but people returning to a language that was always theirs.

Most language tools start from zero. Kin Lantern starts from your comprehension. We know you already have instincts, an ear, a history with the language — so we build from there toward real conversations with the people who matter most.

  • Meets you at your actual level — from overhearing to talking confidently
  • Builds toward real conversations with parents and grandparents
  • Culturally grounded, not just linguistically accurate
  • A shame-free space to practice, available whenever you want
